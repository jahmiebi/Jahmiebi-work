Over its history as food, the uses of honey are for cooking, baking desserts, as a spread on bread, as an addition to other beverages such as tea, and as a sweetener in some commercial beverages.

Due to its energy density, honey is an important food for virtually all hunter-gatherer cultures in warm climates, with the Hadza people ranking honey as their favourite food.
